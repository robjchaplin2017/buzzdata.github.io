Ericsson technology drives the automotive software innovation
Share on Twitter Share on Facebook Share on LinkedIn Share on Mail

Drawing on our long heritage of innovation within telecommunications, we can help you capture the full potential of automotive IoT. From connectivity management and IoT integration, to software and app development, we can help you improve (or completely transform) your offering.

Ericsson Connected Vehicle Cloud connects you with vehicles, drivers and passengers around the globe. Connect Vehicle Marketplace allows you to make the most of those connections, with services including infotainment, telematics and a digital car key. Connectivity Management services ensure those services are always accessible and at top-performance. Click here to learn more.

We help car makers navigate the fast-evolving connected vehicle landscape.

Volvo connected cars

Volvo connected cars make life safer, more efficient and more entertaining.

LYNK & CO delivers

Born digital, LYNK & CO delivers a car that is always connected to create a new automobile experience.

Scania pioneers

Scania pioneers platooning with vehicle to vehicle communication to improve fleet efficiency and reduce their carbon footprint.

Why Ericsson for connected vehicle solutions?

Drawing on our long heritage of innovation within telecommunications, we can help you capture the full potential of automotive IoT. From connectivity management and IoT integration, to software and service development, we can help you improve (or completely transform) your connected vehicle. Ericsson enables automakers and fleet owners to develop, deploy, and manage secure and reliable connected vehicle services worldwide.

End-to-end partnerships

Start small or go big we provide full support for your connected vehicle offering.

Experience and insight

Set the pace for automotive IoT with our leading-edge technology.

Robust
data security

Keep proprietary data flowing securely around the world.

Future-proof solutions

Integrate legacy technology and stay open to new developments.

Our Connected Vehicle Solutions provide everything you need to build, integrate, monetize and manage a connected vehicle ecosystem. We will meet you wherever you are on the road to automotive IoT, with services to quickly achieve current goals, and strategies to keep moving forward.
