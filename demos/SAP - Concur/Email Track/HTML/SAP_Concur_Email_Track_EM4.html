Manage employee-imitated spend with an integrated solution.
 
Consumers are embracing new ways to purchase, with modern technology shifting toward e-commerce and services available at the click of a button.
Siloed data isn’t useful when you’re trying to gain a broad view across your organization’s spend and finances — after all, you can’t control what you can’t see. A complete T&E solution needs to track spend data from all channels — including all the partners, all the data, all the systems and all the people who are doing the spending — but should also work efficiently with your other systems via seamless integration.
A fully integrated T&E solution provides:

  • Integration with apps your travelers use every day, such as Uber and Airbnb.
  • Transfer of data to and from your ERP or accounting solution.
  • Automatic updates of data model changes between systems.
  • Electronic receipts direct from key travel vendors.
  • Ability to identify new cost savings via easy third-party solution integration.
